🚨 Issue #1: Zero Conversions

Severity: Critical - immediate action required

🔎 Root Cause Analysis:

  • Conversion Tracking: LinkedIn Insight Tag may not be firing properly on thank-you page

  • Form Friction: Too many fields or technical issues preventing submissions

  • Message-Market Fit: Ad copy may not align with landing page expectations

  • Ad Format Testing: Experiment with carousel and single image formats
